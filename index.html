Situated in the heart of Isan. A small quiet province in the northeast that is birth many Isan cultures and identities, this place is filled with traditional Isan cultures, customs and ways of living despite years of being oppressed and discrimated by Bangkok. Roi Et to Isan idendity is what Prescott is to Cowboy identity. This is where the wedding will take place. Our wedding date is 11th February 2023. We are excited to see you there!

Travels

To Thailand

Best way to travel to Thailand is to fly to Bangkok - The capital of the country. You will probably need a layover and that could make the total travelling time longer than what a French person works in a week. Anything around 1000 - 1500 USD returns is reasonable. You will get a visa exepmt up to 30 days with US passport.

In Thailand

Thailand offers a wide range of public transportation at affordable prices, we will arrrange transport from Roi Et to Khao Yai then to Koh Kood should you'd like travel with us, you can also rent a car for about 50 USD per day, famous providers includes Herz, 24Hourcars and many more!


We recommend flying from Bangkok directly to Roi Et, AirAsia offers a flight between 30 to 60 USD one way. From Roi Et, we will take a bus through the heartland of Isan Area to Khao Yai, it's about 5 hours drive. Here we will arrange transpotation so please let us know if you are coming to Khao Yai with us From Khao Yai to Koh Kood, we have already arranged a pick up service which includes bus & boat. It is a full-on door-2-door service. In Koh Kood, you can also arrange a transfer from Koh Kood Beach Resort back to Bangkok or anywhere else in Thailand
